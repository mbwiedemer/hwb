Overview

Herbert Woody Blair, Jr. was the seventh child of Herbert Woody Blair, Sr. and Francis Elizabeth Wilson. He mostly grew up in the small town of Muleshoe, in west Texas.

His parents and siblings called him “Junior” but most everyone else knew him as Herb.
